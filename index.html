Minneapolis

Minneapolis, the state's most populous city, spreads out on a relatively level plain. Within its limits are 22 lakes and lagoons and some 170 parks

Minneapolis is popular for it's vibrant ar scenes:

  1. Minneapolis Institute of Arts.
  2. Frederick R. Weisman Art Museum.
  3. Walker Art Center and Minneapolis Sculpture Garden.
  4. Guthrie Theater./li>
  5. Minneapolis Chain of Lakes Regional Park.
  6. Midtown Global Market.
